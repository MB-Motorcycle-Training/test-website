A Full Unrestricted Licence

£1200
  • Age Requirement: Age 24 (or 21 if candidate has held a full A2 licence for 2 yrs)
  • Bike Specification: Minimum 595cc Motorcycle with at least 40 kw (54 bhp)
  • Includes: An Introductory Session (lasts 60-90 minutes), CBT, Module 1 training, one Module 1 test fee, and 7 sessions of Module 2 training (each session 2½ - 3 hours), and one Module 2 test fee.
  • Extras: Additional sessions (2.5–3 hrs): £150

A2 Full Restricted Licence

£1200
  • Age Requirement: Age 19-23
  • Bike Specification: Minimum 395cc motorcycle at least 25kw/34 bhp. Not exceeding 35 kw (47 bhp)
  • Includes: An Introductory Session (lasts 60 to 90 minutes), CBT, Module 1 training, and one Module 1 test and 7 sessions (each session 2½-3 hours), and one Module 2 test fee.
  • Extras: Additional sessions (2.5–3 hrs): £150
